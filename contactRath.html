About this website:

This website has been compiled from information that is readily available from business premises, on the internet, in newspapers, on community notice boards and in the public domain. If you find any incorrect information or would like some information to be removed, please don’t hesitate to get in contact.

If you would like to have details of your business or group added to this website, please email us with the details.

If you haven't found the information that you expected to see on this website, please get in touch so it can be added, if possible.

Connect with your community:

This website can be used to help people connect.

E.g. if you’d like to find people to help form a group or put together a project, this website can help to bring people together. You can send your details to our email address and we’ll do our best to help make connections and to put the word out without you having to put any personal details on the internet.
